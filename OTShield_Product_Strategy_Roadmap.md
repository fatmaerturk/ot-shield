# OTShield Product Strategy & Roadmap
## Industrial Cybersecurity Platform for Critical Infrastructure Protection

---

## Executive Summary

**OTShield** is a comprehensive Industrial Cybersecurity Platform designed to protect critical infrastructure from evolving cyber threats. Our platform combines advanced threat detection, real-time monitoring, and compliance management to secure Operational Technology (OT) environments.

### Market Opportunity
- **Global OT Security Market**: $44.59B (2025) → $78.25B (2032) | CAGR: 8.4%
- **Target Market**: Energy, Manufacturing, Transportation, Healthcare, Water & Wastewater
- **Addressable Market**: $7.8B in target segments
- **Competitive Advantage**: Integrated platform with AI-powered detection and compliance automation
