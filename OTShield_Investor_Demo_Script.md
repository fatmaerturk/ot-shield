### **2. Dashboard & Real-time Monitoring (8 minutes)**

#### **Login & Authentication**
*"First, let me show you our secure authentication system. We use JWT tokens and role-based access control."*

**Actions:**
- Navigate to login page
- Demonstrate secure login
- Show role-based dashboard access

#### **Main Dashboard Overview**
*"Welcome to our unified dashboard – the command center for OT security operations.

In critical infrastructure, every second matters. That’s why our platform provides real-time metrics, a live threat feed, and an advanced PCAP analysis feature for deep, retrospective investigations – all in one place.

Let’s start with a clean PCAP file containing normal IEC104 traffic. Instantly, the dashboard generates key network insights: Protocol Distribution, Top IPs, Top Ports, plus a dynamic visualization of the network topology and detailed packet-level information.

Notice that the Indicators of Attack (IOA) tab is empty – confirming there are no threats present.

Now, let’s switch to live traffic monitoring. With just a click in the Live Network Traffic tab, the system begins streaming real-time data, giving operators immediate visibility into their network.

Finally, we upload an attacked PCAP file. Here’s where the power of our platform shines: the Indicators of Attack tab instantly lights up, revealing detected attack types. Even more, each identified attack is mapped directly to the MITRE ATT&CK framework, which you can explore in the MITRE ATT&CK Matrix tab – providing industry-standard context and actionable intelligence for faster, more precise response.

This isn’t just monitoring – it’s about empowering OT teams to detect, analyze, and neutralize threats before they escalate, ensuring the safety and reliability of critical operations."*
