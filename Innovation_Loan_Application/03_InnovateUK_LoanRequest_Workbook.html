We protect critical national infrastructure — utilities, transport, manufacturing, defence — by deploying realistic decoy assets that mimic real Programmable Logic Controllers, RTUs, HMIs and SCADA endpoints, combined with a Safetech-operated UK threat-intelligence fabric that has captured 12,968 attacks from 157 distinct threat actors across 20 countries.
